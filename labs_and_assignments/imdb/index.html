All about Celeste

Celeste is a platformer game where the player takes on the role of Madeline; a young girl who, after a major anxiety attack, drops everything in her life and travels to mystical Mt Celeste in order to attempt to climb to the summit. Along the way she learns about herself, processes trauma, and makes a handful of friends including:

  • A strange woman who lives on the mountain and guides her on her journey
  • A young american influencer with a passion for photography
  • A kindly but volitile ghost who runs an abandoned inn
  • A part of herself that she is running from, given physical form by the powers of the mountain

The game is a tough as nails platformer with a forgiving learning curve and many additional challenges that, through its gameplay and cutscenes, explores themes of anxiety, depression, dysphoria, and coping with loss.
